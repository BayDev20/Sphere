WebGL Subdivided Sphere Viewer

Instructions

Use your mouse to click and drag the sphere to rotate it in 3D space. Use the slider to zoom in and out. Click the "Change Colors" button to randomize the colors of the sphere's triangles and edges.

About the Sphere

This interactive 3D sphere is created using WebGL and represents a recursively subdivided polyhedron. The base shape is a tetrahedron, and each triangular face is subdivided recursively to approximate a sphere. This process, known as subdivision, results in a smooth, rounded shape made up of many smaller triangles.
